Exist// Do Not

Originally submitted to the Visions juried showcase in analogue form, Exist//Do Not has been transcribed into the Ink engine for digital accessability. In its original form, the book required you to keep track of your stats, and allowed you to lie if you wished. While this has not been preserved in the digital copy, forthcoming updates will alter the text to respond to your previous choices.
