Narrative Games

Exist// Do Not

Originally submitted to the Visions juried showcase in analogue form, Exist//Do Not has been transcribed into the Ink engine for digital accessability. In its original form, the book required you to keep track of your stats yourself, chastising you if you lied or 'cheated.' While this feature has not been preserved in the digital copy, forthcoming updates will implement more responsive text.

Roles: Narrative design, formatting, 'programming.'

Traditions

A tabletop RPG I've been working on in my spare time, Traditions will allow players to develop the mechanical and cultural aspects of their very own 'Tradition', or magical order. While the full game itself isn't finished yet, I have posted a ten-page preview of some rules I've developed for playtesting, overviewing the card-based action economy, spellcasting system, and the Destruction school of magic. When the first round of playtesting finishes, I'll likely release an alpha version to the public.

Roles: Narrative design, mechanics design, formatting.

Other Games

Softbodies

Softbodies was my attempt at making the cutest, simplest mobile game I could manage. Because of its simplicity, I was able to implement key UI elements without much difficulty. The postmortem is available online: however, I did add enviornmental sounds and anti-death spiral mechanics in a recent update. In the future, I plan to introduce an 'infinite mode' to procedurally generate levels, and publish the final release to the android store. For a video preview, please visit the itch.io page.

Roles: UI design, 2D artist, level design, mechanics design, programming.'
