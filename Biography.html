Currently a game design student at Fitchburg State University, and a staff writer for Nerfwire.com.
I'm also working at a gas station, but we don't have to talk about that.
I grew up reading and playing TRPGs like Dungeons and Dragons, and remain an avid Dungeon Master. (Or Game Master, if you're a copyright lawyer.) Much of my current design philosophy has evolved from DMing, and it's given me a lot of experience in constructing branching narratives.
While I would still primarily consider myself to be a writer, I have experience in production, mechanics design, level design, UX/UI design, technical art, 2D art, 2D animation, and a modest amount of programming: after all, while narrative design is an important part of many games, I don't think it's feasible to have that as my only skill. Sometimes, people just want to shoot stuff.
I'm also not actually a dog.
