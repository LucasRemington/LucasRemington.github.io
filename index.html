My principles of design:

1. I strive to express some aspect of myself through every piece I create.

2. I try to learn something new from every piece I create, and make every piece as perfect as I possbily can.

3. I show respect to the intelligence of my audience, never putting myself or my work above them.

4. Good art either entertains or enlightens. Great art does both. I strive to create great art, though I do not always succeed.

5. Art can be created in any medium. However, different mediums require different approaches to create the best possible art.

6. What sets games apart from other mediums is the players ability to make meaningful choices.
