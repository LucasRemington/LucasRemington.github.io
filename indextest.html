Exist// Do Not

A text-based game, which was exhibited in the Visions juried showcase at Fitchburg State University. I wrote and designed the entire piece by myself.

While this game was intended to be presented in an analog format, I have also created a digital copy. I do plan to take advantage of the new digital format, but as of now, it's pretty much an exact copy of the original.
