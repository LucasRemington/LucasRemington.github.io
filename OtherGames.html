About Me

Softbodies

Softbodies was my attempt at making the cutest, simplest mobile game I could manage. Because of its simplicity, I was able to implement key UI elements without much difficulty. The postmortem is available online: however, I did add enviornmental sounds and anti-death spiral mechanics in a recent update. In the future, I plan to introduce an 'infinite mode' to procedurally generate levels, and publish the final release to the android store. For a video preview, please visit the itch.io page.

Team Size: 1

Development Time: ~3 weeks, early 2018

Roles: Sole developer.

Music Credit: TJ Mitchell

Gyro's Escape

An infinite runner originally developed for PC, Gyro's Escape was recently ported over to Android. As a member of the original development team, I was asked to assist with the port, and contributed all 2D assets used in the final game. For a video preview, please visit the itch.io page.

Team Size: 3

Development Time: ~2 weeks, early 2018 - ported late 2018

Roles: 2D artist.

Team Members: TJ Mitchell, and Valeera Davis.

Guardian's Awakening

Guardian's Awakening is a 2.5d platformer set in a dying world: as a Golem, a supposed guardian of the garden, you traverse through the ruined and spider-infested wreckage of the place you were created to protect, collecting mementos of the past. While my primary contribution to this project was level design, I also all of the dialogue and text popups, and was responsible for organizing and directing my other team members. For a video preview, please visit the itch.io page.

Team Size: 4

Development Time: ~4 weeks, early 2018

Roles: Level designer, 2d artist, producer, narrative designer.

Team Members: Sam Gomes, Dakota Lyons, and Valeera Davis.
